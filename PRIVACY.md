# Privacy Policy for TikTok Analyzer

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

## 1. Information Collection
This App connects to your TikTok account via official OAuth2 protocols. It fetches public trend data and your own video metrics (likes, views, shares).

## 2. Data Usage
Data is processed locally on your machine. We do not store your data on external servers. Metrics are used solely to provide you with insights into your posting performance.

## 3. Data Protection
Your API keys and access tokens are stored locally in a `.env` file on your machine. You are responsible for keeping this file secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App uses the TikTok Display API and RapidAPI. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for how they handle data on their end.

## 5. Your Rights
Since all data is local, you can delete your data at any time by deleting the local database or environment files.
